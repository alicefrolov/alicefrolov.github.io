01 About

I write and research at the intersection of cellular aging, epigenetic reprogramming, and longevity science — synthesizing primary literature into structured, open-access publications.

My work focuses on the molecular mechanisms that underlie biological aging: senescence, NAD⁺ decline, epigenetic drift, and the emerging science of age reversal. I publish openly through OSF with permanent DOIs, and run Voveca Research, an independent initiative mapping the longevity science landscape.

I approach these questions through close reading of primary sources and systematic synthesis — the same rigour expected in academic review, made accessible to a scientific audience.
