01 About
Science Writer · Unaffiliated
Science Writing Scientific Illustration Longevity Biology

I write, research, and illustrate at the intersection of cellular aging, epigenetic reprogramming, and longevity science — synthesizing primary literature into structured, open-access publications.

My work spans three modes: science writing that translates complex biology for scientific audiences; independent research — systematic reviews and analytical essays built from close reading of primary sources; and scientific illustration, producing original figures that visualise molecular mechanisms and biological concepts.

I publish openly through OSF with permanent DOIs, accessible to the global research community without paywalls.

Currently completing secondary school, in preparation for undergraduate studies in biology.
