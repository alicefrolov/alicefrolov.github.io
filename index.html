01 About
Photo

I write and illustrate at the intersection of epigenetics, chromatin biology, and cellular aging — synthesising primary literature into structured, open-access publications hosted on OSF.

My work spans science writing and scientific illustration, with a focus on making complex molecular biology accessible without sacrificing rigour. I produce original figures for publications and visual essays covering epigenetic mechanisms, aging pathways, and chromatin-level regulation of gene expression.

Methodological focus: literature synthesis · computational annotation · figure design. All publications are openly accessible with permanent DOIs.
