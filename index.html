Biography

Currently preparing for undergraduate admission in Biotechnology at Universidad de Buenos Aires (UBA). Completing secondary education requirements through adult secondary school (2026–2028) while studying Spanish at UBA and developing foundational knowledge in molecular biology, aging research, and longevity science.

My academic focus centers on understanding the molecular mechanisms of aging and exploring potential interventions to extend healthy lifespan. I approach this through systematic literature review, exploratory writing, and documentation of emerging research in cellular senescence, epigenetic reprogramming, and computational biology.
