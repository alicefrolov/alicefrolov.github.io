01 About

I write and research at the intersection of epigenetics, DNA methylation, and cellular aging — synthesising primary literature into structured, open-access publications hosted on OSF.

My work currently spans science writing and scientific illustration. I am building toward a research career in epigenetics — undergraduate in biology, then a PhD focused on chromatin remodelling or epigenetic clocks.

I publish openly with permanent DOIs, accessible without paywalls.
